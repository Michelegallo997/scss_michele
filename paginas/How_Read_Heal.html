Reading before bed has several benefits, both for the mind and the body.

Improved sleep: Reading can help you disconnect from daily worries and prepare your mind for rest.

Stress reduction: Reading can be an effective way to relax. A study from the University of Sussex found that just six minutes of reading can reduce stress levels by 68%, more than other relaxing activities such as listening to music or taking a walk.

Increased concentration and memory: Reading regularly before bed can improve concentration and memory, as it stimulates brain activity and promotes attention span.
